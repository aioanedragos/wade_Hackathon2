Parents from a city want to create a secret santa for their kids. A parent give gift to another child and one parents are the Santa and deliver the giths. 
To organize this the mayor propose SurprisedKids application to register every child and every parent interested to be the santa for the kids. 
The parents  register their kids in the application with some information and have chance to be Santa for this event. The application store kid  information and 
parents candidation. After completing the registrations an algorith choose a kid for every parent and chose the Santa. Server send information about road-condition, wether, etc. 
to help Santa with this activity.
